Our story

2005-2010 2010-2015 2015-Till Date

Founded in 2005 in Lagos, Nigeria, NovaTrack began with a clear vision—to revolutionize vehicle tracking, quickly establishing itself as a reliable innovator in fleet management.

Expanded comprehensive solutions, introducing real-time GPS tracking, geofencing, AI-powered monitoring, and smart IoT solutions, becoming a leader in regional logistics.

NovaTrack is a recognized market leader providing cutting-edge fleet management solutions, comprehensive IoT integrations, and customized analytics trusted by major brands nationwide.

Vision

To revolutionize the fleet management and IoT solutions industry by delivering cutting-edge technology that empowers businesses to optimize operations, reduce costs, and improve customer satisfaction.

Mission

To provide innovative, customer-centric solutions that streamline fleet operations, enhance productivity, and drive business growth, while maintaining the highest standards of quality, integrity, and reliability.
